# 🎬 Fan Companion — Demo Script

**Duration:** 3-5 minutes  
**Setup:** Phone + laptop (or second phone)

---

## 🎯 Demo Hook (15 seconds)

### What You Say:
> "Imagine watching Steph Curry break Ray Allen's 3-point record, but you don't know what a 'pick-and-roll' is, you've never heard of Ray Allen, and you're not sure why everyone's so excited. That's the casual NBA fan experience."

### What You Show:
- Open the app on your phone
- Hold it up so audience can see

---

## 📱 Act 1: The Problem (30 seconds)

### What You Say:
> "The NBA has 5-6 'tentpole' games per year — Christmas, All-Star, Finals — where even casual fans tune in. But these fans are **lost**. The commentary is full of jargon. The players are strangers. The records are context-free."

### What You Show:
- Point to the login screen
- "This is our AI companion for those fans."

---

## 🏀 Act 2: The Setup (45 seconds)

### Step 1: Login
**Action:** Enter your email (any email works, it's mock auth)  
**What You Say:**
> "Sign in with your NBA ID. For the demo, we're using mock auth."

### Step 2: Game Selector
**Action:** Show the 4 game cards  
**What You Say:**
> "Pick a legendary moment. We've curated classic games — tonight, we're watching Steph break the all-time 3-point record at Madison Square Garden."

**Action:** Tap the Curry game card

### Step 3: Catch-Up Tab
**Action:** Show the Catch-Up content (scroll through storyline, rivalry, watch-for)  
**What You Say:**
> "Before the game starts, the companion catches you up. Here's the storyline — Steph needs just 2 threes to pass Ray Allen. The rivalry — MSG has always been Curry's stage. What to watch for — Ray Allen is in the building."

---

## 📺 Act 3: Second-Screen Mode (90 seconds) **[MAIN DEMO]**

### Step 1: Explain Second-Screen
**Action:** Show the Second-Screen Mode panel (📺 icon, "Start Watching" button)  
**What You Say:**
> "Here's the magic. You're not watching the video in this tiny phone screen. You're watching on **TV, YouTube, or League Pass** — this is your second-screen companion."

### Step 2: Open YouTube (on laptop or second device)
**Action:** Click "Watch on YouTube" link, or manually go to:  
`https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J6RT3BHHvPU`

**What You Say:**
> "I've got the game playing here on my laptop. Let's say I tune in at this moment..."

**Action:** Scrub to ~2:30 in the video, note the score on screen  
(Example: Warriors 7, Knicks 9, Q1 at ~8:22 for the record-tying shot)

### Step 3: Manual Sync
**Action:** On phone, tap **"Start Watching"**  
**What You Say:**
> "I click 'Start Watching' and tell the companion what I see on screen right now."

**Action:** Enter the score you noted:
- Away (GSW): `12`
- Home (NYK): `16`
- Quarter: `Q1` (tap Q1 button)
- Click **"Jump to play"**

**What You Say:**
> "Warriors 12, Knicks 16, first quarter. The companion syncs instantly."

### Step 4: Auto-Advance
**Action:** Watch the app — it should jump to the matching play  
**What You Say:**
> "Now watch. The feed auto-advances every 8 seconds to match the game pace. I don't have to do anything."

**Action:** Let it advance 2-3 plays (~20 seconds)  
**Point out:**
- NOW card updating
- Score changing in header
- Green "Following game • Auto-advancing" status

**What You Say:**
> "See? It's following along. The score updates, new plays appear, all synced to what I'm watching on the big screen."

---

## 🎨 Act 4: The Features (60 seconds)

### Feature 1: Jargon Translator
**Action:** Wait for a play with a blue pill button (or tap a past play that has one)  
**Example plays with jargon:**
- "Alley-oop" — play #6 (Q1, 6:22)
- "Pick-and-pop" — play #18 (Q2, 9:20)

**What You Say:**
> "Okay, 'alley-oop' — what is that? I tap here..."

**Action:** Tap the blue jargon pill  
**What You Show:** Translation card appears with plain-language explanation

**Read it aloud:**
> "A pass caught mid-air and dunked — a perfectly timed aerial handoff.' No basketball PhD required."

### Feature 2: Player Lookup (Legacy Lens)
**Action:** Wait for a play with a red pill button (or tap a past play with a player)  
**Example:** Any Curry play

**What You Say:**
> "Who's Curry? I tap here..."

**Action:** Tap the red player pill → switches to Legacy Lens tab  
**What You Show:**
- Player detail card (name, number, bio)
- Career stats grid (2,972 career 3PM, 1 behind record)

**What You Say:**
> "Two-time MVP, three-time champion, greatest shooter ever. And look — he entered tonight ONE behind Ray Allen's record."

### Feature 3: Milestone Alert
**Action:** If the app hasn't reached the milestone yet, scrub the YouTube video forward to ~Q1 5:58 (the record-breaking shot)  
**Re-sync if needed** (tap "Re-sync" button, enter new score)

**What happens:** Milestone overlay appears (🏆 HISTORY MADE)

**What You Say:**
> "And here it is — career three #2,974. The app knows. Full-screen milestone, stat callout, continue button."

**Action:** Tap "Continue" to dismiss

### Feature 4: Ask AI
**Action:** Switch to "Ask AI" tab  
**What You Say:**
> "And if you still have questions, just ask."

**Action:** Type a question, e.g.:  
`why is breaking this record such a big deal?`

**What You Show:** Wait for Claude response (~2-3 seconds)  
**Read response aloud** (paraphrase):
> "Ray Allen held the record for 10 years. Curry built a championship dynasty around the 3-pointer. It's like breaking Babe Ruth's home run record."

## 🎬 Demo Tips

### Before You Start:
- ✅ Charge your phone (100%)
- ✅ Open YouTube video on laptop (cued to ~2:00)
- ✅ Have your deployed URL bookmarked
- ✅ Test the sync once beforehand (know the score at 2:30)
- ✅ Close other apps on phone (clean screen)

### During Demo:
- **Speak to the audience, not the screen** — hold phone up so they can see
- **Pause for reactions** — when milestone hits, let them see it
- **If sync drifts:** Just tap "Re-sync" and correct — say "this is why we have manual override"
- **If AI is slow:** Say "Claude is thinking..." and keep talking

### After Demo:
- **Share the link:** "Try it yourself — scan this QR code" (optional: have a QR code ready)
- **Take questions:** Common ones below

## 💬 Common Questions & Answers

### Q: "Does this work with live games?"
**A:** "Not yet — we're in Phase 1 with classic games. Phase 2 adds live game support via NBA API. Same UX, real-time data."

### Q: "What about betting?"
**A:** "We're not a betting platform. This is education and engagement — helping casual fans understand the game, not predict it."

### Q: "How accurate is the sync?"
**A:** "Manual sync is exact — you tell it the score, it finds the right play. Auto-advance is within 1-2 plays over 10 minutes, and you can re-sync anytime."

### Q: "Why not just embed the video?"
**A:** "YouTube restricts NBA video embeds. But more importantly — fans don't watch games on their phone. They watch on TV. This is built for that."

### Q: "What's the AI model?"
**A:** "Claude Sonnet 4 from Anthropic. We send the current game state (score, quarter, last play, player roster) with every question, so responses are contextually aware."

### Q: "Can I add my own games?"
**A:** "Yes — we have tools to pull play-by-play from the Kaggle nbadb dataset, write catchup content, add player bios, and map video timestamps. Takes about 2 hours per game."

### Q: "What about other sports?"
**A:** "The architecture is sport-agnostic. We'd need NFL/MLB/Soccer play-by-play data and jargon dictionaries, but the companion itself works for any sport."
